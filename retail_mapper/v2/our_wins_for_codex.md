agreement is now 92.7%. The remaining 33,725 disagreements include 5 areas
where the full pipeline made better calls. Codex should adopt these.

## 2. Frozen vegetables stay in Frozen (3,960 SKUs)

Storage-department matters for shopping. Frozen mixed vegetables, frozen
peas, frozen broccoli are sold in the FROZEN aisle, not Produce.

**Sample disagreements:**
```
fdc      title                              full → codex
1486962  FROZEN MIXED VEGETABLES            Frozen > Vegetables → Produce > Vegetables
1554337  FROZEN PEAS                        Frozen > Vegetables > Peas → Produce > Vegetables > Peas
2168936  FROZEN BROCCOLI FLORETS            Frozen > Vegetables > Broccoli → Produce > Vegetables > Broccoli
```

**Rule:** when title has "FROZEN" or BFC is "Frozen Vegetables" or
"Frozen Prepared Sides", route to `Frozen > Vegetables`, not `Produce`.

## 3. Pasta dinners in Meal (1,163 SKUs)

Mac & Cheese boxes, Pasta Roni, Hamburger Helper — these are shelf-stable
meal kits, not frozen single-entrees.

**Sample disagreements:**
```
fdc      title                                    full → codex
2495039  KRAFT MACARONI & CHEESE DINNER            Meal > Pasta Dishes → Frozen > Single Entrees
1467405  PASTA RONI, ANGEL HAIR PASTA WITH HERBS   Meal > Pasta Dishes → Frozen > Single Entrees
```

**Rule:** when BFC is "Pasta Dinners" or PI matches "Pasta Roni" / "Mac and
Cheese" pattern, route to `Meal > Pasta Dishes`, not `Frozen > Single Entrees`.

## 4. Nut & Seed Butters in Pantry (463 SKUs)

Codex incorrectly routes peanut/almond/cashew/sunflower/seed butters to
`Dairy > Butter`. They belong in `Pantry > Nut Butters` — they're shelf-
stable spreads, not dairy.

**Sample disagreements:**
```
fdc      title                              full → codex
2089708  CREAMY PEANUT BUTTER               Pantry > Nut Butters > Peanut Butter → Dairy > Butter
2675084  ALMOND BUTTER, CRUNCHY             Pantry > Nut Butters > Almond Butter → Dairy > Butter
2210482  CASHEW BUTTER                      Pantry > Nut Butters > Cashew Butter → Dairy > Butter
1554371  SUNFLOWER SEED BUTTER              Pantry > Nut Butters > Sunflower Seed Butter → Dairy > Butter
```

**Rule:** when title contains "(peanut|almond|cashew|sunflower|hazelnut|
seed) butter" OR "tahini" → route to `Pantry > Nut Butters > {Specific Butter}`,
NOT `Dairy > Butter`.

## 5. Canned vegetables stay in Pantry (524+ SKUs)

Codex pushes canned vegetables to fresh Produce. They belong in Pantry
(canned-goods aisle) — title explicitly says "canned" or "in syrup" or
"in juice" or has BFC indicating canned/shelf-stable.

**Sample disagreements:**
```
fdc      title                              full → codex
2455552  GREEN BEANS, CANNED                Pantry > Canned Vegetables > Green Beans → Produce > Vegetables > Beans
2210479  CANNED CORN                        Pantry > Canned Vegetables > Corn → Produce > Vegetables > Corn
2495255  CANNED CARROTS                     Pantry > Canned Vegetables > Carrots → Produce > Vegetables > Carrots
```

**Rule:** when BFC is "Canned Vegetables" or title contains "canned" /
"in syrup" / "in juice" / "in brine", keep in `Pantry > Canned Vegetables`,
NOT push to `Produce`.

   full pipeline → ~99% agreement.
